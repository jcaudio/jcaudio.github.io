Outdoor Cinema FAQ

 Does it have to be dark?

Yes, we advise starting at sunset although if you are in a shaded area like woods then you can sometimes start earlier, around dusk.

 How much space do I need?

For our standard rear projection set-up we need an area 3m x 6m. We ask that you don’t seat any of the audience closer to the screen than 2m as, just like in a normal cinema, they will be too close.

 How do you weigh the screen down?

We provide 10kg sandbags for each leg of the projector screen to ensure stability. We can screen in wind speeds up to 25knots. After that we are afraid that we will have to take the screen down for health and safety reasons.

 Can we put stuff on the screen before the film?

Yes no problem. We often show shorts, adverts or – if your event has a sponsor – we can show a video or slide to promote them. If you choose the paramount package, our onsite technician will be able to assist and seamlessly transition between pieces of content for you.

 Do we need power?

The indie package needs 1 domestic 13 amp socket. We can provide adequate power cabling to help you run your screen wherever you like.
The paramount package usually needs 2 domestic 13 amp sockets, but depending on your audio solution of choice, could need additional power. Please contact us and we can advise further.
